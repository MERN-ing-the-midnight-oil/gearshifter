Consignly

Software for nonprofits running consignment gear swaps—bike swaps, ski swaps, and more—so day-of runs smoothly and wrap-up is painless.

What you will be able to do

  • Run events end-to-end — create swap dates, registration windows, and pickup windows; archive events when you are done.
  • Onboard sellers — invite links and registration forms you can tailor; sellers list items from their phones (or web where configured).
  • Check-in and labels — staff check-in flows with QR-friendly item tracking and label printing for the sales floor.
  • Point of sale without taking cards — mark items sold in the app while your org uses Square, cash, or whatever you already use at checkout.
  • Pickup and payouts — track what is paid at pickup (including optional check references) so consignors and accounting stay aligned.
  • Reports and inventory — export CSV for accounting and manage organization inventory after the event (storage, resale, donations).
  • Sale alerts — push notifications when a seller’s item sells so they can celebrate (or plan pickup) in real time.

Who it is for

Built first for local nonprofits that already volunteer-run swaps—and designed to grow into a broader multi-tenant product when you are ready to scale beyond a pilot.
