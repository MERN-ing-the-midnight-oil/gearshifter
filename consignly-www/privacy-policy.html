Privacy policy

This policy describes how information is handled when you visit consignly.org (the public marketing and “coming soon” site for Consignly). It does not govern a separate product or mobile app, which will have its own notice when offered.

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Who we are

Consignly is operated by Rhys J Smoker. For privacy questions about this website, you may reach out via the project’s GitHub repository.

What this site collects today

This site is a small static page hosted on GitHub Pages. There is no account sign-up, no contact form, and no intentional collection of personal data on the page itself. Like most websites, hosting infrastructure may automatically process technical data (for example IP address, browser type, date and time of request) in server or platform logs for security and reliability. GitHub’s handling of that data is described in GitHub’s privacy statement.

Cookies and similar technologies

This marketing site does not set its own analytics or advertising cookies. Fonts may load from Google Fonts; Google’s terms and privacy practices apply to that request. You can read more at Google’s Privacy Policy.

How we use information

Any technical data processed by the host is used only to deliver the site, protect services, and comply with law. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

We do not maintain a separate database for visitors of this static site. Retention of server or platform logs, if any, is determined by GitHub and similar infrastructure providers under their policies.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, or to object to certain processing. Because this site does not intentionally collect identifiable information from visitors, there may be nothing for us to retrieve. You may still contact us as above and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy when the site or product changes. The effective date at the top will be revised when we do.

This page is provided for transparency about the current marketing site. It is not individualized legal advice. When Consignly ships as a product with accounts and event data, you should publish a fuller policy tailored to that service and your jurisdictions.
